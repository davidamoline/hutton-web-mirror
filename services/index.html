What We Offer

Periodontal & Implant Services

Complete specialist care — from early gum disease to complex full-arch reconstruction.

Service 1 of 14

Periodontal Health

Healthy gums are the foundation of everything.

Expert diagnosis and treatment of gum disease using minimally invasive techniques — from early prevention to advanced periodontitis care.

  • Comprehensive periodontal screening and risk assessment
  • Scaling and root planing (deep cleaning)
  • LANAP laser gum therapy — no scalpel, no sutures

Service 2 of 14

Bone & Tissue Grafting

Rebuild what's been lost. Restore what's possible.

Advanced regenerative procedures that rebuild bone volume and restore gum tissue — the critical foundation for implants and long-term oral health.

  • Socket preservation after tooth extraction
  • Alveolar ridge augmentation for implant readiness
  • Sinus lift procedures

Service 3 of 14

Implants & Restoration

Permanent tooth replacement that looks, feels, and functions naturally.

Precision-guided dental implant placement and full-arch restoration using 3D imaging, digital planning, and biologic integration techniques.

  • Single-tooth and multi-tooth implant placement
  • Full-arch restoration (All-on-X)
  • Implant-supported dentures and bridges

Service 4 of 14

Advanced Treatment

Full-arch. Full-smile. Full transformation.

Complex full-mouth rehabilitation, All-on-X procedures, and interdisciplinary treatment planning for patients who need comprehensive reconstruction.

  • All-on-4 and All-on-X full-arch implant therapy
  • Immediate-load implant protocols
  • Interdisciplinary treatment coordination
Learn More →

Service 5 of 14

Jawbone Health

Structural integrity you can feel — and see.

Diagnosis and treatment of jawbone conditions affecting implant success, facial structure, and long-term oral health.

  • Jawbone density evaluation and diagnostics
  • Pre-implant bone assessment and planning
  • Treatment of osteonecrosis and pathologic bone loss
Learn More →

Service 6 of 14

Prophylaxis (Teeth Cleaning)

A clean mouth is a healthy mouth.

Professional dental prophylaxis removes plaque and tartar buildup to halt gum disease progression and keep your smile healthy.

  • Removes plaque and tartar above and below the gum line
  • Halts the progression of gingivitis and periodontal disease
  • Eliminates surface stains for a brighter smile
Learn More →

Service 7 of 14

Scaling & Root Planing

The foundation of non-surgical periodontal treatment.

Scaling and root planing is a thorough, non-surgical deep cleaning that removes bacterial deposits from beneath the gum line to stop periodontal disease.

  • Removes plaque and tartar from beneath the gum line
  • Smooths root surfaces to prevent bacterial reattachment
  • Non-surgical treatment for gingivitis and periodontitis
Learn More →

Service 8 of 14

Bone Grafting

Restore the foundation that makes implants possible.

Major and minor bone grafting procedures rebuild jawbone volume lost to disease, extraction, or injury — restoring the structure needed for dental implants and a healthy bite.

  • Rebuilds bone lost to disease, extraction, or injury
  • Socket preservation at the time of extraction
  • Sinus bone grafts for the upper posterior jaw
Learn More →

Service 9 of 14

Gum Disease Laser Therapy

Advanced treatment — no scalpel, no sutures, no anxiety.

Laser therapy for periodontal disease offers precise, minimally invasive treatment with less bleeding, faster healing, and reduced post-operative discomfort.

  • No scalpel or sutures required
  • Reduced bleeding and swelling
  • Virtually painless — anesthesia often minimized
Learn More →

Service 10 of 14

Bite Adjustment

A balanced bite protects your teeth for life.

Bite therapy corrects destructive bite patterns that cause tooth wear, loosening, and jaw joint damage — preserving your teeth and restoring comfortable function.

  • Eliminates uneven bite pressure points
  • Custom bite splint therapy available
  • Addresses TMJ discomfort and jaw joint stress
Learn More →

Service 11 of 14

Gum Grafting

Protect exposed roots and restore your gum line.

Gum grafting reconstructs receded gum tissue, covering exposed roots, eliminating sensitivity, and protecting teeth from further recession and decay.

  • Covers exposed root surfaces
  • Eliminates root sensitivity to hot and cold
  • Stops and prevents further gum recession
Learn More →

Service 12 of 14

Guided Bone & Tissue Regeneration

Rebuild what disease has taken — without compromise.

Guided bone and tissue regeneration uses surgical membranes and biologics to stimulate the regrowth of bone and connective tissue lost to periodontal disease.

  • Regenerates bone and attachment lost to gum disease
  • Stabilizes teeth at risk of loss
  • Prepares the jaw for dental implant placement
Learn More →

Service 13 of 14

Cosmetic Periodontal Surgery

A beautiful smile starts with the gum line.

Cosmetic periodontal procedures reshape the gum line, cover exposed roots, and restore lost jaw contours — giving your smile the proportions and framing it deserves.

  • Crown lengthening to correct a gummy smile
  • Soft tissue grafting to cover exposed roots
  • Ridge preservation after tooth loss
Learn More →

Service 14 of 14

Oral Cancer Exam

Early detection saves lives.

Annual oral cancer screenings by our specialists can detect pathologic changes at the earliest, most treatable stage — using advanced laser technology and clinical expertise.

  • Completely painless examination
  • Laser-assisted visualization of subsurface changes
  • Detection of red patches, lesions, and leukoplakia
Learn More →
