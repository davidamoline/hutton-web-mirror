About Us

A Practice Built on Precision

Board-certified periodontists. Microsurgical technique. Outcomes that speak for themselves.

Our Philosophy

Excellence in Every Detail

Oral Plastic Surgery was founded with a straightforward mission: to deliver the highest standard of periodontal and implant care available in Southern California. Over more than three decades, we have remained committed to that standard — not because it is easy, but because our patients deserve nothing less.

Our approach is grounded in evidence-based dentistry, minimally invasive technique, and genuine patient partnership. We do not rush. We do not overtreat. We listen, we explain, and we build a treatment plan that reflects your specific anatomy, goals, and concerns.

We also remain active educators and researchers in our field. Our doctors have lectured nationally and internationally, contributed to peer-reviewed journals, and trained the next generation of periodontists. That intellectual engagement keeps us at the forefront of what is possible — and it directly benefits every patient we see.

Our team at the American Academy of Esthetic Dentistry annual meeting.

Recognition & Education

Active in Our Field

Our doctors are not only practitioners — they are contributors to the profession. They lecture nationally, participate in continuing education at the highest levels, and engage with the research and clinical communities that advance periodontal and esthetic surgery.

This commitment means our patients benefit from techniques and materials that are current, proven, and backed by the most rigorous clinical evidence available. When you choose Oral Plastic Surgery, you are in the care of doctors who are genuinely invested in the state of their field.

Why Choose Us

What Sets Us Apart

Board-Certified Specialists

All three of our physicians hold board certification from the American Academy of Periodontology — a credential that fewer than half of practicing periodontists maintain.

Microscope-Enhanced Surgery

We use high-magnification surgical microscopes for all soft tissue procedures — enabling precision at a level invisible to the naked eye.

Minimally Invasive Approach

Wherever possible, we employ laser therapy, flap-free technique, and biologic grafting materials to shorten recovery and improve long-term tissue response.

Collaborative Care Model

We work closely with your general dentist, prosthodontist, and other specialists. You get a coordinated plan, not isolated procedures.

Research-Active Clinicians

Our doctors contribute to the published science of their field. That commitment to evidence keeps our clinical protocols current and our outcomes consistent.

Patient-Centered Experience

We explain everything. We involve you in every decision. And we take calls from our own patients after surgery — because genuine care doesn't end at checkout.
