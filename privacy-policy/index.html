Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Oral Plastic Surgery ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information you provide to us through this website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily submit through our contact and appointment request forms, including your name, email address, phone number, and any messages or health information you choose to share. We also collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, browser type, general geographic region) through our website analytics platform. This data is aggregated and not linked to individual users.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule appointments
  • Provide information about our services
  • Improve our website and patient communications

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Medical Privacy

Any health information you share through this website will be treated with strict confidentiality consistent with our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices. Please see our HIPAA Notice for full details on how we handle protected health information.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and gather aggregate analytics data. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; this will not affect your ability to use our site.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information you submit against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@oralplasticsurgery.com or (858) 459-7374.
